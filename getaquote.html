Get a quote on your house portrait

Email me to commission a portrait of your home. I'll need a high resolution photo of your house, so think about what angle you like it to be drawn from. If possible, try to get a photo with a good light source; shadows and sun glare can distort the image and make it difficult to draw.

Prices vary, depending on the size of the work and the complexity of the house. The average range is $250 - $400. Email me or phone me and we can discuss your needs. Phone: (612) 607 4784

Framing is separate. I work with local framing shops in the Twin Cities, but you may wish to frame the work yourself.

Shipping is also separate. I can deliver in the Twin Cities metro area in Minnesota, but I can also ship your house portrait by post if you live farther away. In the case that your portrait is delivered via post, I would recommend that your arrange framing yourself, after delivery.
