I have a dear friend, Michael, for whom I wanted to get a special gift so I immediately thought of Alex Geer and his beautiful house portraits. I drove to Mike's house, snapped pictures from many different angles. I sent Alex my favorite image and indicated which elements were important to include in the final work (in this case it was the old lamp post).

Alex's pen and ink drawing of the home was beautifully rendered, but just as importantly, it was warm and personal... literally. It took my breath away when I first saw that gorgeous work of art. Michael was ecstatic and obviously touched.

We had note cards printed up of this drawing and I have a shopping bag and face masks printed with images of my own home, also created by Alex. It's the gift that keeps on giving! I can't think of a more meaningful gift for a friend, family member or customer!

– – Diane C. Prince
